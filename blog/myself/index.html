Profile

I am a full stack web and mobile developer and systems administrator based in Accra, Ghana.

I have been building websites and applications for the past two years and I graduated with my first degree from Switzerland Management Center University (SMCU) with a Bachelor in IT-Security (BBA). I am currently pursuing my second degree in BSc. (Hons) Computer Science at Regent University College of Science and Technology

I have worked with the Electricity Company of Ghana for the since 2013 as an IT Assistant. My work in the company has helped in shaping me to be a well rounded human resource since my schedules traverse into other departments such as Engineering, Finance, Human Resources, just to name a few.

In my spare time I like to create my own websites to keep up with the latest web technologies and I also like playing soccer and teaching others about web development.
